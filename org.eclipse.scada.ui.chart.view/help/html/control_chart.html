Control the chart

Pan

In order to pan press and hold the CTRL key, point the cursor to the starting point on the chart and click-drag to start panning. Release the mouse button to stop.

Zoom

There are currently two ways to zoom:

Mouse wheel

Use the mouse wheel for zooming in and out the X-Axis (time).

Hold the CTRL key when scrolling the mous wheel in order to zoom in and out the Y-Axis (values).

Click & drag

Click and drag with the mouse on the start. With the starting click a rectangle is drawn that defines the zoom ratio.

Dragging the rectangle from upper left to lower right will zoom in.

Dragging the rectangle from lower right to upper left will zoom out.
